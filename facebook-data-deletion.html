Data Deletion Instructions

This page explains how users can request deletion of data associated with their use of our mobile application through Facebook Login or other Facebook services.

Data We Collect

Our application may receive basic profile information from Facebook, such as a unique Facebook User ID, name, and profile picture, solely for the purpose of authentication and gameplay functionality. We do not sell or share Facebook user data with third parties.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to request deletion of your Facebook-related data, please follow the steps below:

  • Send an email to feelfrolic@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: Facebook Data Deletion Request
  • Include your Facebook User ID and the name of the application

What Happens After You Submit a Request

Upon receiving a valid data deletion request, we will remove all Facebook-related user data associated with your account from our systems within 30 days, unless retention is required by law.

If we do not store any Facebook-related data for your account, we will confirm that no data is retained.
